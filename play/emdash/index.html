Crafting tactile expressions of brand philosophy

I created a range of brand applications for EMDASH, including brand cards, packaging, and delivery boxes. To reflect EMDASH’s core concept — finding clarity within ambiguity — thick tracing paper was chosen as a key material, adding depth and tactility to the unboxing experience. This material choice communicates the brand’s subtle tension between transparency and concealment.

Brand Background

Wearing a message that sparks change

EMDASH is a Korean fashion brand that embeds messages within clothing, encouraging people to express their beliefs and inspire positive change through what they wear. Each collection turns garments into statements — transforming everyday fashion into a medium for self-expression and social dialogue.

Project Goal

Delivering brand values through packaging

The goal was to design EMDASH’s brand card and packaging, enclosed with each product shipment. The packaging was intended to make the brand’s concept visually tangible — allowing customers to immediately sense EMDASH’s identity and philosophy the moment they receive their order.

Solution

Expressing mystery through form and material

To visualize EMDASH’s philosophy of delivering hidden messages through fashion, embossed typography and semi-opaque tracing paper were combined to evoke a sense of intrigue and discovery. The design invites viewers to look closer — revealing meaning through layers, much like the brand itself.
