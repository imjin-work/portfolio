Revealing the human cost behind urban development

This poster was created for Project 88, a collaboration with the graphic studio Fax.community. The photo collage portrays residents displaced during the construction of the 1988 Seoul Olympic Stadium. A taped “X” references the marks placed on homes designated for demolition — a stark reminder that it was not only buildings, but people, who were erased in the name of progress.
