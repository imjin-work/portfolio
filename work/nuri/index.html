A public cultural support card for underserved communitie

The Seoul Culture Nuri Card is a prepaid card issued by the Korean government to support basic cultural access for low-income individuals, including recipients of basic livelihood assistance and near-poverty groups. It enables users to participate in cultural activities such as the arts, local travel, and sports, helping ensure that essential cultural experiences remain accessible to all.

Solution

Bringing warmth and clarity to the renewed mascot

The character was rebuilt in 3D to add a sense of liveliness — creating a mascot that feels animated and approachable across promotional materials. Elements representing the benefits of the Culture Nuri Card were integrated into the poster design, making the card’s purpose intuitive and easy to understand at a glance.
